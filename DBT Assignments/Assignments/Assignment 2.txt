                                Introducing Relational Databases



1) Which field of the Customers table is the primary key?

The primary key of the Customers table is Cnum.

It uniquely identifies each customer in the table, so no two customers can have the same Cnum.



2) What is the 4th column of the Customers table?

The 4th column in the Customers table is Rating.

It represents the rating or score assigned to a particular customer.



3) What is another word for row? For column?

A row is also called a record or tuple.

A column is also known as an attribute or field.



4) Why isn’t it possible to see the first five rows of a table?

In a relational table, rows are not stored in any guaranteed order.

Because of this, the concept of “first five rows” only makes sense when an ORDER BY clause is used.
